Scratch Project

Computer Science Principles

September 3, 2019 - September 11, 2019

My partner and I had to program a game with increasing levels of difficulty using Scratch. The main challenges we had to overcome were figuring out the errors in our code, thinking of other ways to write our code, and working out how to make our game actually work smoothly without any glitches. Something I learned from completing this project is to prioritize and focus on doing the most important things first before moving on to the little, less important details. A few skills I picked up along the way were time management, organization, and how to communicate and collaborate with a partner.
